Glider Pilot Logbook

Glider Pilot Logbook is a web application for glider pilots in the United Kingdom. It helps you view, add, and edit flight records in your own Google Spreadsheet on Google Drive. The spreadsheet stays your official logbook; this website is the interface and automation layer on top of it.

Purpose of this application

  • Manage a gliding flight logbook through a web and mobile-friendly interface
  • Read and write flight rows in your connected Google Sheets logbook via Google APIs
  • Show dashboard totals, medical dates, legality, and flying statistics
  • Optionally allow a participating gliding club to import missing flights into your spreadsheet, only with your consent

You sign in with Google so Glider Pilot Logbook can access the Google account and spreadsheet files needed to provide these features. See the Privacy Policy and Google Drive access pages for details.
