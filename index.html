Glider Pilot Logbook

Glider Pilot Logbook is a web application for glider pilots. It helps pilots record flights, manage their digital logbook, monitor pilot currency and medical certificates, and review flight statistics.

Users sign in with their Google Account to securely access their personal logbook stored in Google Sheets.

The app uses your Google account email and profile for sign-in, and Google Drive access only for the spreadsheet and files you choose or create through the app (optional broader Drive access applies only if you use automatic logbook setup).
